Introduction

Vita Contracting ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Project details and requirements
  • Communication preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

Website Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website information
  • Device and operating system information

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing construction services and project management
  • Communicating about your project and responding to inquiries
  • Sending project updates and important notifications
  • Processing payments and managing billing
  • Improving our website and services
  • Complying with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted subcontractors and suppliers involved in your project
  • With payment processors for transaction processing
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

The Sustainable Community Center represents a new standard for environmentally conscious public construction in Austin.
